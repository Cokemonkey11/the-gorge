Description

A speedy, unmanned airship just recently brought into service by the Dwarven Air Force. Armed with a D35 Artillery Improv and a stockpile of FrenchClaw missiles, this new model already shows promise, both as an air superiority fighter, and as a powerful reconnaissance machine.

Hmm.. Another Scratch Made model, created using Milkshape 3d and Magos' Model Editor. This one took me about 5 hours of work over 2 days.

In this download, You'll get models of both the Airship, and the Missiles it uses in it's Attack Slam animation. The missile model is not recommended for the normal attack of the model.
